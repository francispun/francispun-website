1 Document Setup & View Modes

Margins: Space between content and the edge of the paper (safe zone for text/content).

Bleed: Usually 0.125 in / 3 mm. Extend backgrounds/images beyond the page edge to avoid white gaps after trimming in mass production.

Tip: Most document settings (margins, bleed, columns) can be changed in the Properties panel when nothing is selected.

4 Images: Container vs. Content

Place image: File > Place or Cmd/Ctrl + D.

Black Arrow (Selection Tool)

Selects the container/frame (blue bounding box).

Resizing affects the frame ‚Üí crops the image.

Content Grabber:

Hover over image ‚Üí double-circle appears in center ‚Üí click it to select content (brown bounding box). Now move/scale image inside frame.

Auto-Fit (Frame Fitting Options):
‚Ä¢ ON ‚Üí resizing frame scales image proportionally.
‚Ä¢ OFF ‚Üí resizing crops.

Fit Frame to Content: Removes excess frame space.

Paste Into: Copy image ‚Üí select shape ‚Üí Edit > Paste Into.

Text Wrap: Window > Text Wrap ‚Üí choose style ‚Üí adjust offset.
