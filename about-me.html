    • About me

    Working hours

    I am an accomplished designer with over a decade of experience in industrial and digital product design. With a deep understanding of user needs and business goals, I am dedicated to creating refined, user-centered solutions that endure and perform.

    I have successfully designed a broad range of products, from kitchenware and audio devices to web and mobile experiences. Proficient in SolidWorks, Figma, Adobe Suite, and front-end development (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript), I combine traditional craftsmanship with modern tools. In today’s AI era, I actively leverage generative AI to accelerate ideation (rapid concept visualization), deepen user and market research, and create interactive UX prototypes—integrating these capabilities seamlessly into both industrial and digital workflows.

    As a versatile, collaborative designer, I partner closely with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders, using research, prototyping, and user testing to deliver intuitive, precisely crafted products that balance aesthetic excellence with commercial success.

    Leisure hours

    Outside of work, my biggest joy is time with my wife and our young son. Whenever I can, I recharge outdoors through hiking, kayaking or camping, and on quieter days at home we watch movies, play, and hang out with our cats.
