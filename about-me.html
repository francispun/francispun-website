    • About me

    Working hours

    I am an accomplished designer with over a decade of experience in product design and user experience. With a deep understanding of user needs and business goals, I am dedicated to creating delightful and user-centered design solutions.

    As a designer, I have successfully designed a wide range of products, including kitchenware, audio devices, and digital products for web and mobile platforms. Proficient in a diverse set of design tools such as Photoshop, XD, Illustrator, InDesign, Figma, and Solidworks, I bring a comprehensive skill set to every project. Additionally, I possess basic web development skills including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

    As a versatile designer, I thrive in collaborative environments, working closely with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders to create intuitive and well-crafted designs. With expertise in UX/UI principles, I employ user testing and prototyping to ensure exceptional user experiences. Proficient in both digital and industrial product design, I utilize a range of industry-standard tools for ideation, visual design, and bringing designs to life.

    Leisure hours

    I find solace in connecting with nature during my leisure time, often engaging in activities such as hiking, camping, kayaking, and paddleboarding. On less favorable days, I indulge in watching movies or spending quality time with my cats.
